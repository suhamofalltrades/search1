About Colossus

Colossus is a metasearch engine built by me, Suham. It aggregates results from multiple search providers:

  • Google
  • Bing
  • DuckDuckGo
  • Yahoo
  • Brave Search

Colossus queries these sources simultaneously and combines the results for a comprehensive search experience without tracking or storing your data.
